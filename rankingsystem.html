The 10 MKOTH RulesTM
S/N Series Rules
1 All games are played in assault mode with classic rules.
2 The starting map is Shapes. The lower ranked player will choose one of the 2 possible starting maps. Either player can invite for the starting map. If there is a dispute over who should invite first, the lower ranked player must invite for the first map, followed by the person selecting the map.
3 The game ends when a player reaches 0 lives, or at the start of round 38. The player with most lives at the end wins. The player who lost in the game will decide the map for the next game.
4 It is considered a draw if both players have same amount of lives by the start of round 38 OR if both players reaches 0 lives before round 38 OR a the same defeat/victory screen is presented on both players screens. If there is a draw, the person who didn't pick the map will invite and select the next map. Games that are drawn do not count as a complete game in a series. For example, in a best of 3 series, if there is 1 win and 2 draws, the series is considered incomplete and both players have to continue playing until a player gets 2 wins. Maps cannot be repeated unless all maps have been played during the series.
5 Each player may choose to ban 1 tower for the entire series, via the MKOTH bot, however all bans will be universal. All powers are allowed. 4th tower may be used if BOTH players agree, otherwise, it is prohibited by default.(refer to rule 7)
6 If one player disconnects during a game, the other player decides whether to rematch, take the win or take a draw.
7 Players have the right to add or amend any rules mentioned above for their series, as long as both players agree. It is encouraged that both players explicitly state the terms in which they play in before the series starts.
8 Players can only do one series with the same person every 12 hours.
9 Players are not allowed to dodge series after it begins. If a player avoids playing the opponent more than 2 times when he is able to, the opponent may claim a forfeit win with manager’s approval. Managers will make a final decision on whether the player is dodging based on chat evidence.
10 Cheating of any kind, such as modding, abusing glitches, hacking, using of macros or spying(battles tv) etc. is strictly prohibited.
