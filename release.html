Arrangements

Entrance of the Gladiators Euphonium Duet

This is an arrangement of a the famous circus music originally titled Entrance of the Gladiators by Julius Fučík from the Romantic Period of classical music. The playing standard of this arrangement is really high as I was making it for my own experiment.

Melody of the Night No.5 Euphonium Solo 夜的鋼琴曲五

This piece of was originally composed by a Taiwanese Composer Shi Jin and one of my friend LSY looks into it to made it into a instrumental track. I really liked this track and asked my friend and I turned it into a Euphonium Solo Arrangement.

Original

那首歌

那首歌 (That Song) was my first complete modern pop culture music, especially in Mandopop music. It was also collaboration with my friend Eddy Chua, who was writing the lyrics for this song. This song is large influenced by the theme music of the movie: 那些年 (You Are the Apple of My Eye), which was very popular at time this was been written.

Night

This was also one of my first completed piano music. This music uses the popular chord progression vi IV I V, exists in many upbeat musics, such as KARA - Step, SNSD - Oh, and Don Omar ft. Lucenzo - Danza Kuduro, but I made into a calm and peaceful piano music.

Electronic

Warning: Just like many common EDMs (Electronic Dance Music), following musics may contain strong language.

Jump Evolution

My first completed electronic music on this powerful mobile platform called caustic 3. I make a very simple approach form music based on a very popular and common chord progression of vi IV I V like I use for one of first piano music.

Euphoria

My first trance music on the same platform. The chord progression is is based on is slightly varied from the one I used for Jump Evolution but just small swap of chords I V positions, to become vi IV V I.
